This is how to make a vegan sandwich!

  1. Get some soft, whole grain bread
  2. Toast the bread (soggy sandwiches are no fun)
  3. Apply some vegan mayo and mix in some hot sauce (light spicy aioli)
  4. Wash some greens and add them in
  5. Add in some vegan meat, i.e., Tofurky!
  6. Add in some vegan cheese if you're feeling spunky
  7. Serve with a side of chips.
